Euler Corrected

   The revised method of Euler, we find the average tg slope of the tangent to two points: x [m], y [m] and x [m +1], y [m] + h * y '[m]. Relations describing this method have the form:

        y[m+1] = y[m] + h*F(x[m],y[m],h)

        F(x[m],y[m],h) = ( y'[m] + f(x[m]+h, y[m]+h*y'[m]))/2

        y'[m] = f(x[m],y[m])

   Corrected Euler method is consistent with the expansion in Taylor series up to terms of degree h ^ 2, as, Thus, Runge-Kutta method of second order.

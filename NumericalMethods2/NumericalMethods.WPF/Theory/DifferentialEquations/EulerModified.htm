Euler Modified

   In this method, we find tg slope of the tangent at:

       x = x [m] + h / 2; y = y [m] + (h / 2) * y'[m]

   Relations describing the modified Euler's method have the form:

        y[m+1] = y[m] + h*F(x[m],y[m],h)

        F(x[m],y[m],h) = f( x[m]+h/2, y[m]+(h/2)*y'[m] )

        y'[m] = f(x[m],y[m])

   The modified Euler method is also consistent with expansion in Taylor series up to terms of degree h ^ 2, and is also the method of Runge-Kutta second order.
